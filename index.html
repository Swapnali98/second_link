Who we are

Grab a Ride is India’s largest mobility platform and one of the world’s largest ride-hailing companies, serving 250+ cities across India, Australia, New Zealand, and the UK. The Grab a Ride app offers mobility solutions by connecting customers to drivers and a wide range of vehicles millions of consumers and over 1.5 million driver-partners.

Grab a Ride Financial Services and a range of owned food brands through India’s largest network of kitchens under its Food business. Grab a Ride was founded in Dec 2010 by Mahadev Pawar and Swapnali pawar with a mission to build mobility for a billion people.
